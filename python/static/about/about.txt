<h2>About HCM</h2>
<h4>Class XII Computer Science Project work (2024 - 2025), for The Hindu Senior Secondary School, Adyar.</h4>
<p>
    This Human Capital Management (HCM) product is developed in Python using the Flask Web Framework and MySQL.
    HCM is for managmenet of employees in an organization. New employees can be onboarded, and their details managed
    using this product. When an employee leaves the organization, he/she can be marked as a leaver.
    Employee records can also be deleted.
</p>
